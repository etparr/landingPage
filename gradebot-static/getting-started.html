Common Questions

Quick answers to help you get started faster.

upload_file What file formats can I upload?

Gradebot accepts PDF files, images (PNG, JPG, HEIC), and Word documents (.docx). For code assignments, you can upload plain text files or use our LMS integration to pull submissions directly.

schedule How long does grading take?

Gradebot grades all submissions in parallel. Whether you have 5 essays or 300, your entire batch completes in 30 seconds to 2 minutes.

edit Can I edit the AI-generated grades?

Absolutely. Click any score to adjust it, edit feedback inline, or rewrite comments entirely. Your expertise stays in the loop—Gradebot handles the heavy lifting while you keep full control.

checklist Do I need to create a rubric from scratch?

No. You can upload an existing rubric or let Gradebot generate one automatically from your assignment prompt. The AI will suggest criteria and point values that you can then customize.

security Is student data secure?

Yes. Gradebot is FERPA compliant. Student submissions are encrypted, never used to train AI models, and can be deleted at any time. We do not share data with third parties.

support Where can I get help?

Pro users get priority email support. Enterprise and district customers have a dedicated success manager for onboarding, training, and ongoing support.
