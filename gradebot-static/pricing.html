Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Gradebot pricing and billing.

help Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your account settings. You will retain access to Pro features until the end of your current billing cycle.

security Is student data secure with the free plan?

Yes. Student privacy is our top priority. We are fully FERPA compliant, and we do not use student submissions to train our public AI models, regardless of whether you are on a free or paid plan.

school Does Gradebot work for all subjects?

Gradebot works across every discipline, from Computer Science and Math to English, History, and Sciences. Whether you're grading essays, code, problem sets, or lab reports, the AI evaluates each submission against your rubric with the same accuracy and transparency.

receipt_long Do you offer purchase orders for schools?

Yes! For the Institution plan, we accept purchase orders and bank transfers. Please contact our sales team to set up an institutional account for your department or school district.
